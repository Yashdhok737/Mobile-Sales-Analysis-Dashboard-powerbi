# 📱 Mobile Sales Analysis Dashboard

### 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a Mobile Sales Analysis Dashboard created using Power BI to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, payment methods, and product trends across different months and cities in India.

The dashboard provides interactive visual insights that help businesses track sales growth, identify top-performing mobile brands/models, and understand customer preferences.

### 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall mobile sales performance.
- Track monthly sales and quantity trends.
- Identify top-performing mobile brands and models.
- Understand customer ratings and satisfaction.
- Analyze transactions by payment methods.
- Visualize sales distribution across Indian cities.
- Monitor weekday-wise sales performance.
  
### 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI – Dashboard creation & data visualization
- Excel – Data source & preprocessing
- DAX – Measures and calculated fields
- Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
  
### 📊 Dashboard Features

#### 🔹 KPI Cards
- Total Sales: 769M
- Total Quantity Sold: 19K
- Transactions: 4K
- Average Sales: 40K

#### 🔹 Monthly Quantity Analysis
- Displays month-wise quantity sold trends.
- Helps identify peak and low-performing months.

#### 🔹 Brand-wise Sales

Comparison of total sales across major mobile brands:

- Apple
- Samsung
- OnePlus
- Vivo
- Xiaomi

#### 🔹 Top Mobile Models

Highlights the top-selling mobile models based on quantity sold.

#### 🔹 Payment Method Analysis

Transaction distribution across:

- UPI
- Debit Card
- Credit Card
- Cash

#### 🔹 Customer Ratings

Visual representation of customer satisfaction ratings.

#### 🔹 Sales by Day Name

Analyzes sales performance across weekdays.

#### 🔹 Geographical Sales Map

Interactive map visualization showing sales distribution across cities in India.

### 📈 Key Insights
- Apple generated the highest total sales among all brands.
- July recorded the highest quantity sold.
- UPI was the most preferred payment method.
- Weekend sales were comparatively higher than weekdays.
- High customer ratings indicate strong customer satisfaction.
